# <a name="view-posted-tax-credit-memos-in-the-new-zealand-version"></a><a name="view-posted-tax-credit-memos-in-the-new-zealand-version"></a>View Posted Tax Credit Memos in the New Zealand Version

You can use the **Posted GST Credit Memo** page and **Posted Purch. Tax Credit Memo** page to view details of GST credit memos and purchase tax credit memos that have been posted.  

The following procedure describes how to view posted GST credit memos, but the same steps also apply for viewing posted purchase tax credit memos.  

## <a name="to-view-a-posted-sales-tax-credit-memo"></a><a name="to-view-a-posted-sales-tax-credit-memo"></a>To view a posted GST credit memo
1. Choose the ![Lightbulb that opens the Tell Me feature.](../../media/ui-search/search_small.png "Tell me what you want to do") icon, enter **Posted GST Credit Memo**, and then choose the related link.  
2. View the FastTabs as described in the following table.  

    |FastTab|Description|  
    |-------------|---------------------------------------|  
    |**General**|General information about the credit memo.|  
    |**Posted GST Cr. Memo Sub**|Posted GST credit memo information.|  
    |**Invoicing**|Invoice information.|  
    |**Shipping**|Shipping information.|  
    |**Foreign Trade**|Foreign trade information.|  

3.  Choose the **OK** button.
